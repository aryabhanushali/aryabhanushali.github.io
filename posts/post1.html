Why I Built This Blog

May 9, 2025 • Arya Bhanushali • Personal, Project

I created this blog as a space to document my learning journey, share insights from my projects, and connect with others who are passionate about AI, neuroscience, and human-centered design.

Building the blog was a hands-on exercise in frontend development. I designed and coded it using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript without relying on pre-made templates. This gave me full control over the layout, animations, and overall user experience.

One of the goals was to make the blog lightweight and simple — each post is just a clean HTML file, making it fast and easy to maintain. I also integrated the blog into my personal portfolio to keep everything in one place.

If you’re curious, you can explore the code on my GitHub repository. Feel free to check it out or fork it for your own projects!
