About Me

Hi! I’m an upcoming third-year Computer Science major at Georgia Tech, specializing in artificial intelligence and human-computer interaction. I’m passionate about applying AI and my technical skills to improve the world around me — whether that means making everyday systems more accurate and efficient or advancing the technology itself to better serve people. Beyond just applying AI, I’m deeply fascinated by the brain and how neuroscience can inspire better, more efficient AI systems. My goal is to help shape the next generation of brain-inspired artificial intelligence, pushing the boundaries of what AI can do while making it smarter, more adaptive, and more human-aligned. Check out my projects to see what I’m building and my blog to see what I’m learning along the way!
